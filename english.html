Our philosophy

Welcome to Voyage Mondial - the restaurant where you can discover a world of flavors. Inspired by a trip around the world, our international theme and diverse menu are meant to take you on a taste journey you won't soon forget. With us, you can experience a dining experience like no other - from spicy Indian dishes and delicious Thai flavors to American classics and world-class Italian cooking.

Our chefs use only fresh and high quality ingredients to create outstanding dishes to suit all tastes and preferences. Our menu also includes vegetarian and vegan options so everyone can enjoy our great food. Whether you want a romantic dinner for two or a party for the whole family, our inviting atmosphere and professional staff are always ready to provide you with the best service and create a memorable experience.

So why not take a break from everyday life and take your taste buds on a trip around the world? Visit us at Voyage Mondial and discover new flavors and cultures in a dining experience you will remember forever.

Our kitchens

Scandinavian

Scandinavian food is known for its simplicity and natural flavors, with a focus on local ingredients from the sea, forest and soil.

Asian

Asian cuisine is known for its diversity of flavors from different regions such as China, Japan, Korea, Thailand and Vietnam.

International

A mix of different cultures and flavors from all over the world. That includes everything from Italian pasta to Mexican tacos.
